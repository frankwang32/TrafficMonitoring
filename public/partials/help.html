Use our website is quite easy!


When first access to our website, you will route into index page. You can press the button on left-top of website to navigate to different pages.


The first page is Map page. You can fill in the form and get a best route of your trip according to incidents data we retrieved from the Internet.


The second page is History page. You can review the searching histories and manage them.


The third page is Hotspot page. You can fill in the form and get traffic incidents drawn as a bunch of markers on the map and view different traffic pattern accordingly.
